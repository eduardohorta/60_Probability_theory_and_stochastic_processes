Kolmogorov’s strong law of large numbers


Let X1,X2,… be a sequence of independent random variables, with finite expectations. The strong law of large numbers holds if one of the following conditions is satisfied:

  1. 1.

    The random variables are identically distributed;

  2. 2.

    For each n, the variance of Xn is finite, and

    ∑n=1∞Var⁡[Xn]n2<∞.
