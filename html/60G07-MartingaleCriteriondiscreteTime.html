Martingale criterion (discrete time)


Theorem.

Let X=(Xn,Fn) be a local martingale. If there is an n0∈N such that ∀n≥n0,n∈N either:

E⁢Xn- <∞

or:

E⁢Xn+ <∞

Then X is a martingale.
