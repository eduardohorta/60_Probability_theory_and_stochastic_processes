independent stochastic processes


Two stochastic processes {X⁢(t)∣t∈T} and {Y⁢(t)∣t∈T} are said to be if for any positive integer n<∞, and any sequence t1,…,tn∈T, the random vectors 𝑿:=(X⁢(t1),…,X⁢(tn)) and 𝒀:=(Y⁢(t1),…,Y⁢(tn)) are independent. This means, for any two n-dimensional Borel sets A,B⊆ℝn, we have

P⁢[𝑿-1⁢(A)∩𝒀-1⁢(B)]=P⁢[𝑿-1⁢(A)]⁢P⁢[𝒀-1⁢(B)].
