hitting time


Let (Xn)n≥0 be a Markov Chain. Then the hitting time for a subset A of I (the indexing set) is the random variable:

HA=inf⁡{n≥0:Xn∈A}

(set inf⁡∅=∞).

This can be thought of as the time before the chain is first in a state that is a member of A.

Wite hiA for the probability that, starting from i∈I the chain ever hits the set A:

hiA=P(HA<∞:X0=i)

When A is a closed class, hiA is the absorption probability.
