Helly-Bray theorem


Let F,F1,F2,… be distribution functions. If Fn converges weakly (http://planetmath.org/ConvergenceInDistribution) to F, then

∫ℝg⁢(x)⁢𝑑Fn⁢(x)→n→∞∫ℝg⁢(x)⁢𝑑F⁢(x)

for each continuous bounded function g:ℝ→ℝ.

Remark. The integrals involved are Riemann-Stieltjes integrals.
