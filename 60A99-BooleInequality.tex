Let $(\Omega,\mathcal{F},P)$ be a probability space and $\{A_1, A_2,\cdots \}$ be a sequence of events 
such that  $\{A_1, A_2,\cdots \} \subset \mathcal{F} $. Then
 $$P(\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty A_n)\leq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(A_n).$$
