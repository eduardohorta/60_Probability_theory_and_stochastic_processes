modification of a stochastic process


Let {Xt}t≥0, {Yt}t≥0 be stochastic processes on (Ω,ℱ,P). {Xt}t≥0 is a modification of {Yt}t≥0 if

P⁢[{ω:Xt⁢(ω)=Yt⁢(ω)}]=1

for all t∈[0,∞).
