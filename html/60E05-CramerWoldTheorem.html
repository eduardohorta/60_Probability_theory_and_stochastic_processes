Cramér-Wold theorem


Let

X¯n=(Xn⁢1,…,Xn⁢k)⁢and⁢X¯=(X1,…,Xk)

be random vectors. Then X¯n converges to X¯ in distribution (http://planetmath.org/ConvergenceInDistribution) if and only if

∑i=1kti⁢Xn⁢i→n→∞𝐷∑i=1kti⁢Xi.

for each (t1,…,tk)∈ℝk. That is, if every linear combination of the coordinates of X¯n converges in distribution to the correspondent linear combination of coordinates of X¯.
