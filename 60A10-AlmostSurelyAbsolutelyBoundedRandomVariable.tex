Let $\{\Omega ,E,P\}$ a probability space and let $X$ be a random
variable; then, $X$ is said to be almost surely absolutely bounded iff a \PMlinkescapetext{constant} $M>0$ exists such that
\[
\Pr \left\{ \left\vert X\right\vert \leq M\right\} =1.
\]
