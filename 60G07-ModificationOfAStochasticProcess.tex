Let $\lbrace X_t \rbrace_{t\geq 0}$, $\lbrace Y_t\rbrace_{t\geq 0}$ be stochastic processes on $(\Omega, \mathcal{F}, P)$. $\lbrace X_t \rbrace_{t\geq 0}$ is a \emph{modification} of {$\lbrace Y_t\rbrace_{t\geq 0}$ if
$$P[\{\omega:X_t(\omega)=Y_t(\omega)\}]=1$$ for all $t \in [0, \infty).$
